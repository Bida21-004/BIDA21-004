GENRE Action / Adventure
RATING: PG-13

Inspired by the acclaimed video game series , this action-adventure epic spans the globe and follows Nate and Sully as they embark on the dangerous pursuit of “the greatest treasure never found” while also tracking clues that may lead to Nate’s long-lost brother
